FAQ

Over the lifespan of our business we have come across a couple of similar questions from our clients and we sought out to show them publicly to enlighten those that require the same help.

Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What kind of events do you manage?
A: We manage a wide range of events, such as corporate events, social events, cultural events, educational events, and more. We can handle any size and type of event, from small meetings to large-scale festivals.

Q: What kind of media services do you provide?
A: We provide a full range of media services, such as event design, branding, marketing, production, digital support, and more. We can help you create a memorable and impactful event identity, as well as promote and showcase your event to your target audience.

Q: How do you charge for your services?
A: We charge based on the scope, complexity, and duration of your event, as well as the media services you require. We offer competitive and transparent pricing, and we can work with any budget. We will provide you with a detailed quote and contract before we start working on your event.

Q: How do you ensure the quality and safety of your events?
A: We ensure the quality and safety of our events by working with a team of experienced and qualified professionals, who have the skills and knowledge to deliver excellence in everything they do. We also follow the best practices and standards of the event industry, as well as the health and safety regulations of the local authorities. We also have a contingency plan and insurance coverage for any unforeseen circumstances.

Q: How do you communicate and collaborate with your clients?
A: We communicate and collaborate with our clients throughout the event planning and execution process, using various channels, such as phone, email, online platforms, and face-to-face meetings. We listen to your needs and expectations, and we provide you with regular updates and feedback. We also welcome your input and suggestions, and we strive to exceed your satisfaction.

Q: How can I contact you or book your services?
A: You can contact us or book our services by filling out the form on our website, calling us at our phone number, or emailing us at our email address. We will respond to your inquiry within 24 hours, and we will arrange a consultation with you to discuss your event details and requirements. We look forward to hearing from you and working with you on your next event.
