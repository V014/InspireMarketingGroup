Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is aligned with the best practices in the field of event and media management. Setting our credibility and delivery of services at the highest they can be.

Privacy Policy

At Inspire Marketing Group, we are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a safe and secure experience when attending our events. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you attend an event organized by Inspire Marketing Group.

Information we collect:
When you register for an event organized by Inspire Marketing Group, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and job title. We may also collect information about your company or organization, such as its name and size. We may also collect information about your attendance at the event, including your session and speaker preferences, as well as any feedback you provide to us. In addition, we may collect information from you if you interact with us on social media platforms.

How we use your information:
We use the information we collect to organize and manage the event, to communicate with you about the event, and to provide you with any related materials, such as presentations, documents, or recordings.

We may also use your information to send you promotional and marketing communications about future Inspire Marketing Group events, products, or services that may be of interest to you.

We may share your information with third-party vendors who provide services to us, such as event registration, badge printing, or audiovisual services. We require these vendors to protect your personal information in accordance with our privacy policy and applicable laws. We will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Security of your information:
We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We restrict access to your information to those who need it to perform their job functions, and we require them to keep your information confidential.

Retention of your information:
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. We will securely delete or destroy your information when it is no longer needed.

Your rights:
You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information that we have collected. You may also object to the processing of your personal information or request that we restrict the processing of your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided below.

Changes to this privacy policy:
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version of the policy will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact us:
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or if you wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at
